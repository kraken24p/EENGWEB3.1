TANA POWER STATION

@Tanapowerstation

Tana power station is in Muranga south district, and is situated 83km from Nairobi on the Nairobi - Nyeri road. As part of its capacity expansion to meet the rising power demand, KenGen has upgraded the station's generation capacity from 14.4MW installed in early 1930's and 1950's to the current 20 MW by constructing a new power station with a capacity of 20 MW.

CUESA EENG CLUB RELEASE

@cuesaeengchuka

The Faculty of Engineering hosted a mentorship talk and officially launched the Chuka University Engineering Students Association (CUESA) under the theme "The Future of an Engineer." The event featured inspiring guest speakers and emphasized the importance of building essential skills, acquiring cutting-edge knowledge, and fostering innovation.

OLKARIA GEOTHERMAL POWER STATION

@olkarianaivasha

Olkaria Geothermal Project is situated within Hell's Gate National Park 120KM from Nairobi and neighbours horticultural farms that produce some of the finest flowers in the world. The project is also adjacent to Lake Naivasha, which is a fresh water lake in the Kenya Rift and a Ramsar site.
